Lasagna

Description

This is a classic easy lasagna that is guaranteed to never disappoint.

Ingredients

  • 1lb of ground beef
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 2 (14 ounce) cans chopped tomatoes
  • 2 (6 ounce) cans tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 teaspoons italian seasoning
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 (8 ounce) package of no-boil lasagna noodles
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella
  • 1 cup ricotta cheese
  • 1/4 cup grated parmesan cheese
  • 6 slices fresh mozzarella
  • chopped parsley

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F
  2. Heat the oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Cook the ground beef, stirring to crumble, until browned, 5 to 7 minutes. Drain off the fat.
  3. To the skillet of ground beef, add the tomatoes, tomato paste, Italian seasoning, salt, black pepper, and garlic powder. Simmer for 5 minutes.
  4. Place half of noodles in a lightly greased 12- x 8- x 2-inch baking dish, slightly overlapping lengthwise edges. Spoon one-third of the meat mixture over the noodles; add half of the shredded mozzarella, ricotta or cottage cheese, and Parmesan cheeses. Repeat layers, and spoon on the remaining meat mixture. Arrange the mozzarella slices on top. Cover the baking dish with foil. Bake at 350Â°F for 35 to 45 minutes. Remove the foil, and continue to bake for an additional 10 minutes.
  5. Let the finished dish rest for 10 minutes before serving. Top with chopped parsley, if desired.
