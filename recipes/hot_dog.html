BLT Hot Dog

Description

BLT hot dogs for an unique touch to an American classic

Ingredients

  • 8 hot dogs grilled to likeness
  • 8 buns
  • 3/4 cups romaine lettuce, chopped
  • 2 small tomatoes seeded and chopped
  • 6 slices bacon, cooked crisp and chopped
  • 1/3 cup mayo

Steps

  1. In a small bowl combine lettuce, tomato, bacon and mayo and stir well.
  2. Allow to sit at room temperature for 15-20 minutes so the flavors combine.
  3. Cook your hot dogs, then top equally with mixture.
